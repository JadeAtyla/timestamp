Payroll Calculation Rules

Understanding how deductions and pay are calculated.

Late Policy

  • • Being late by 1 minute = 15 minutes deduction
  • • Additional minutes after the first minute are added to the 15 minutes
  • • Example: 5 minutes late = 19 minutes deduction (15 + 4)

Work Hours Calculation

  • • Expected work start time: 8:00 AM
  • • Total hours worked = Time Out - Time In
  • • Undertime = Expected Hours - Actual Hours Worked
  • • Both late time and undertime are deducted from pay

Pay Calculation

  • • Gross Pay = Hours Worked × Hourly Rate
  • • Deductions = (Late Minutes + Undertime Minutes) ÷ 60 × Hourly Rate
  • • Net Pay = Gross Pay - Deductions + Bonus
  • • Calculations are updated daily and summed by payroll period

Payroll Cutoff Types

  • • 15th and 30th: Two payrolls per month (1st-15th, 16th-30th/31st)
  • • Weekly: Monday to Sunday payroll periods
  • • Daily: Payroll calculated daily
